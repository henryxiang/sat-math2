### Question 22

A company offers you the use of its computer for a fee.
Plan A costs <span>$6</span> to join and <span>$9</span> per hour to use the computer.
Plan B costs <span>$25</span> to join and then <span>$2.25</span> per hour to use the computer.

----

### Answer 22

### Question 23

If the probability that the Giants will win the NFC championship is p and
if the probability that the Raiders will win the AFC championship is q,
what is the probability that only one of these teams will win its respective championship?

----

### Answer 23

$$p(1-q) + q(1-p) = p + q - 2(pq)$$

### Question 24
